JLM Healthcare

JLM Healthcare, with its concentration on senior housing, is a full service nursing home owner and operator with a focus on care of higher acuity patients. JLM currently owns 10 facilities in Illinois, which is comprised of 1,553 beds and is actively pursuing new investments in this sector.

JLM Healthcare Mission Statement: To create an environment for our business and shareholders that attracts and retains committed, innovative, caring personnel that serve the needs of each resident, family member, and employee.
