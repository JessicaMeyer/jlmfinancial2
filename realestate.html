Real Estate

2525 McKinnon, Dallas, TX

JLM's Real Estate division and its principals acquire and manage both fee simple assets and loans in all areas of commercial and residential real estate. We have made recent investments in Texas, Arizona, Colorado, Georgia, Illinois and Florida that include retail, industrial, medical, multifamily, self-storage, flex, senior housing and office buildings.

Acquisition Criteria: We acquire assets nationwide, with a preference in the South, Southeast, Midwest and Atlantic Coastal areas. Our priority assets are multifamily, retail, office, medical, senior housing, industrial, self-storage and flex.

Development Criteria: During opportunistic market cycles, we will pursue ground up development with a special interest in multifamily, high rise luxury condominiums, and high rise luxury office buildings.

Plaza Turtle Creek, Dallas, TX

Plaza Turtle Creek II, Dallas, TX

During periods of economic expansion, JLM has developed and co-developed new properties in Texas including uxury Condominiums, Class A apartments, and office buildings including:
