Meet The Team

Larry Meyer | Managing Partner

Mr. Meyer has over 35 years of experience in commercial and residential real estate investments and development, acquisition and management of both REO and CRE loans, corporate mergers and acquisitions and franchising. He has served on numerous corporate boards and has a long history of philanthropic work. Mr. Meyer is currently the Chairman of the Strategic Planning Committee for the Boys & Girls Club of Greater Austin, Texas.

Jimmy Nassour | Managing Partner

Mr. Nassour, a licensed attorney in the State of Texas, has over 25 years of experience in multiple areas of the real estate industry. In addition to significant experience investing in real estate secured assets, Mr. Nassour is considered an expert in matters of real estate law (including finance, acquisitions, closings, property title, and taxation).

Pat Laffey | Chief Financial Officer

Mr. Laffey, a Certified Public Accountant (CPA), has over 30 years of direct taxation and accounting experience in the real estate industry. He holds a degree from Duke University and a Master’s of Science degree in taxation from the University of Baltimore. As Chief Financial Officer for JLM Financial Investments, Mr. Laffey directs the firm’s financial goals, objectives, and budgets. Mr. Laffey’s responsibilities also extend to Managing JLM Healthcare as well as managing JLM’s financial risks, supervising cash management activities, participating in capital raising activities, and providing support on potential acquisitions.

Jeff Ezell | Managing Director

Mr. Ezell also has a Bachelor of Science from Texas Tech University.
